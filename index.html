Welcome to the Anglican Parish of Kapiti

Our worshipping communities are located in the suburbs of Paekakariki, Raumati Beach and Paraparaumu Beach, and are made up of Christians of all ages and stages on their walk with God. The expressions of worship we offer through our services reflect our Anglican heritage, but persons of any or no faith tradition are welcome to worship with us.
