Church Services

Sunday Morning

St Marks Church, Rosetta Rd, Raumati Beach
8:00am: BCP Eucharist, First Sunday of each month
9.30am: Family Eucharist every Sunday but the fifth Sunday

St Paul's Church, 1 Langdale Ave, Paraparaumu
8.30am: Eucharist (third Sunday uses the BCP liturgy)
10:00am: Family Communion with Children’s Programme
Both services every Sunday except the fifth.

St Peter's Church, Beach Rd, Paekakariki
10:00am: Family Eucharist every Sunday but the Fifth

Fifth Sundays are usually a Parish Combined Congregation Service which moves around the churches. Please check the newsletter for details.

Sunday Evening

Choral evensong at St Mark's
5:00pm: 2nd Sunday of the month

Messy Church at St Paul's
Usually 4th Sunday of the month, 4-6pm
Currently not active and being reconceived.

Stillpoint- A contemplative service at St Paul's
Usually in the 1st Sunday of the Month
Currently in Abeyance. Will be reconsidered in 2021.

Friday Midday Eucharist

Every Friday at St Paul's, 12 noon
A short Eucharistic service.

All our services are open for anyone of any age to come and join in. Reading related Children’s activities are available at all Sunday morning services.

Wider Church Online Service - Sundays at 10:00am

Live stream here: Anglican Movement Online. Also available on Shine TV, Youtube and Facebook.
